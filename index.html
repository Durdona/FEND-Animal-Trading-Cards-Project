Akhal Teke Horse

One of the most beautiful horses Akhal-Teke has a reputation for speed, endurance, intelligence, and a distinctive metallic sheen.

  • Country of origin: Turkmenistan
  • Breed: Stands between 58' & 64' high
  • Colors: Palomino, cremello or perlino
  • Uniqueness: Brilliant metallic sheen coat, intelligent, loyal

The Akhal Teke stallion, Absent, won the Gold medal in Individual Dressage at the 1960 Rome Olympic Games, and by the end of his career won five medals in three Olympic games with three different riders.
